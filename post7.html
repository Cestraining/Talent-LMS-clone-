BOSS BEAUTIES® FINANCIAL LITERACY TRAINING BEGINNER COURSE

Mission Statement We are extremely excited to present to you the Boss Beauties® Academy! This virtual education platform is a safe and non-judgemental place to learn financial literacy. Our proprietary curriculum was designed to educate students from Beginning - Advanced levels of learning.

Prepare for Success Master essential finance-related topics from financial responsibility, budgeting, and investing in real estate- with our self-paced courses built by those who value a quality education, and those who are passionate about delivering it to underserved individuals.

Vision/Value Statement

The vision that guides our collective endeavors is of a society in which women of color are afforded genuine opportunities to thrive and achieve financial freedom regardless of their circumstances or location. In upholding our vision, we believe that the development of the field of financial literacy must be guided by an unwavering commitment to excellence, equity, and access.

The following commitments will guide this program's pursuit of excellence.
Student-centered Approach:
- Student support is intensive and individualized.
- Program design is dynamic and responsive.
- Program accommodates students' different learning styles and specific needs.
- Program articulates the course of study with long-term pathways to further education .
- Program seeks and integrates student input into the program.
- Students have opportunities to cultivate social, professional and leadership skills.
